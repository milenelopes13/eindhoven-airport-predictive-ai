1. AI customer profile groups

Created from measurable passenger behaviour, satisfaction and spending indicators.

Efficient Business Traveler

Low dwell time, lower retail focus, high need for speed and convenience.

Marketing use:Fast Track, business parking, lounge-style offers.

Holiday Leisure Traveler

Longer dwell time, medium spend, often travels during peak/holiday periods.

Marketing use:Duty-free bundles, family food offers, seasonal campaigns.

Retail-Oriented Passenger

High store spend, higher dwell time and strong interest in food/retail offers.

Marketing use:Restaurant vouchers, store discounts, loyalty promotions.

Experience-Risk Passenger

Lower satisfaction, higher complaint likelihood, more sensitive to queues/service issues.

Marketing use:Recovery vouchers, service follow-up, targeted support.

Enter a new passenger profile and the AI predicts the most likely customer group.

3. Marketing recommendation engine

The predicted customer group is translated into practical marketing actions for Eindhoven Airport.
