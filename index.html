Predictive AI Customer Group Analysis

This tool uses measurable Eindhoven Airport KPIs to create customer profile groups, link them to marketing actions, and predict which group a new passenger is most likely to belong to.

1. AI customer profile groups

The groups are based on measurable KPIs: customer satisfaction, staff service score, non-aviation spending, purchase frequency and complaint likelihood.

High-Value Passenger

High spend, frequent purchases and strong satisfaction.

Airport improvement: Increase non-aviation revenue through targeted retail and loyalty offers.

Loyal Passenger

High satisfaction, strong staff service score and low complaint likelihood.

Airport improvement: Maintain service quality and encourage repeat airport use.

Revenue Opportunity Passenger

Positive experience but low spend or low purchase frequency.

Airport improvement: Use promotions to increase airport store purchases.

At-Risk Passenger

Lower satisfaction, weaker staff service score or higher complaint likelihood.

Airport improvement: Improve service recovery and reduce negative feedback.
