Predictive AI Passenger Segmentation

This AI creates customer profile groups, connects them to marketing actions, and predicts the likelihood that a new passenger will belong to each group.

1. AI customer profile groups

The customer groups are based on passenger behaviour, spending, satisfaction and service experience.

Business Traveler

Travels for work, values speed, low queue time and efficient service.

Marketing use: Fast track, business parking, lounge offers.

Leisure Traveler

Travels for holidays or family trips, responds well to airport experience offers.

Marketing use: Family deals, food offers, holiday campaigns.

Retail-Oriented Traveler

Spends more in non-aviation stores and makes more purchases at the airport.

Marketing use: Duty-free discounts, store bundles, restaurant vouchers.

Experience-Risk Traveler

Lower satisfaction, longer queues or weaker service experience.

Marketing use: Recovery vouchers, support messages, service improvement actions.
